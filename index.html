MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPLE


Success comes to those who work hard and stays with those, who don’t rest on the laurels of the past.
Teamwork is the hallmark of St. Francis’s School. A committed and supportive management, dedicated teachers, caring and co-operative parents blend harmoniously to create a child-centric school. It is natural to find in this ambience, the intensive use of a variety of thinking activities, strategies and group dynamics so that the classrooms become alive. I am very sure through collaborative effort we can achieve more to benefit our students who are the future leaders of tomorrow. We are indeed grateful to the parents for reposing year after year such complete confidence and entrusting their children to us to mould them to worthy citizens of the society.

Principal
(PUSHPENDRA SHARMA)
